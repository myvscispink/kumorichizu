Who is Kumorichizu

Kumorichizu is an aspiring artist and passionate anime fan, currently navigating the journey of creativity and self-expression. As a student, she’s fueled by her love for art and all things anime, striving to bring her dreams to life through her unique creations.

The name "Kumorichizu" comes from a special memory — inspired by a delicious cheesecake she had while visiting Kumori, Japan. The name blends the essence of her artistic journey and the sweet inspiration she draws from her travels, with "Kumori" meaning "cloud" and "chizu" meaning "map" or "chart"— guiding her toward new artistic horizons.

At Kumorichizu, we believe in the power of fandom to bring people together. We want you to express your love for anime in the most fun and stylish way possible!

Bringing anime magic to your everyday life, one sticker at a time!
